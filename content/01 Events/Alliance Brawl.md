# Alliance Brawl

**Type:** Rotating alliance PvP/raid event
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium-High — replaces [[Alliance Mobilization]] post-KvK for eligible kingdoms

## What it is
An alliance-vs-alliance raid-style event involving caravan mechanics and loot rotations. Timing your caravan hits and loot collection is the core skill here.

## Core Strategy
- **Caravan timing** — caravans move on a schedule; hitting them at the right window matters more than raw troop count
- **Loot priorities** — coordinate with officers on which caravans/targets to prioritize rather than everyone hitting the same one
- **Raid rotations** — this event often runs in waves; pace yourself rather than dumping everything into the first wave

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Confirm caravan/raid schedule with officers before the window opens
- [ ] Don't burn all your attempts/speedups in the first wave — pace across the rotation

## Teaching point for new members
> Alliance Brawl is about timing caravan hits and following the loot rotation your officers call out — don't just charge in randomly, and don't blow everything in the first wave.
