# Alliance Championship

**Type:** Weekly competitive alliance event
**Priority:** 🟠 High — weekly, needs coordination for best rewards

## What it is
A weekly PvP-style event where your alliance competes as a unit. Formation discipline and role assignment matter a lot more here than raw individual power.

## Core Strategy
- **Troop ratio baseline:** ~50/20/30 (Infantry/Cavalry/Archer as a starting point — adjust based on what the meta favors that season)
- **Lane strategy:** commonly run as a "2-1" split — coordinate with officers on which lane you're assigned to rather than freelancing
- Buff timing matters — coordinate popping alliance buffs together rather than everyone using them individually

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Know your assigned lane/role before the event starts (this is an officer/R4 job to assign)
- [ ] Use the recommended troop ratio baseline, not your rally ratio
- [ ] Check the shop for that week's priority items before spending points elsewhere

## Common mistakes
- Members freelancing into whichever lane looks easiest instead of following the assigned split
- Wasting alliance buffs early/individually instead of syncing timing
- Ignoring the Alliance Championship shop — season rewards there can be high value

## Teaching point for new members
> Alliance Championship is a weekly team event — the officers will assign you a lane, use roughly a 50/20/30 troop mix unless told otherwise, and don't pop buffs on your own timing. Coordination matters more than your personal power here.
