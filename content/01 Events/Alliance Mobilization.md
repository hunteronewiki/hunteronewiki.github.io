# Alliance Mobilization

**Type:** Rotating alliance event, typically following KvK
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium — good alliance coordination reward, situational

## What it is
A post-KvK alliance event focused on collective activity/participation. Once a kingdom becomes eligible for [[Alliance Brawl]], Alliance Mobilization is **replaced** by Alliance Brawl in that slot — Alliance Mobilization then only reappears after the next Strongest Governor event.

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Check whether your kingdom is currently on the Alliance Mobilization or Alliance Brawl track (ask officers if unsure)
- [ ] Treat it like other alliance events — coordinate timing and participation with your alliance rather than going solo

## Teaching point for new members
> Alliance Mobilization usually shows up after KvK — but if your kingdom has unlocked Alliance Brawl, that takes its place instead. Ask an officer which one is currently active if you're not sure.
