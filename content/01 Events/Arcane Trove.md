# Arcane Trove

**Type:** Rotating 5-day chest event
**Priority:** Medium — mainly relevant if you're considering spending

## What it is
A 5-day chest-opening event with tiered pack pricing building toward a Grand Prize unlock.

## Key Rules
- The Grand Prize unlocks after reaching a specific spend/opening threshold — check the in-game progress bar before buying any pack to see how close you actually are
- Compare the pack price directly against what it returns before buying — not all tiers are equal value

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Check the Grand Prize threshold before spending anything
- [ ] Treat this as an optional spend event, not a F2P priority — the free portion is worth claiming but the paid tiers are situational

## Teaching point for new members
> Arcane Trove is a chest event mainly worth engaging with if you're planning to spend — claim whatever's free, but check the Grand Prize threshold before buying any packs.
