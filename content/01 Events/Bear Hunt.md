# Bear Hunt

**Type:** Fixed weekly alliance event
**Priority:** 🔴 Non-negotiable — one of two "can't get materials anywhere else" weekly events (the other is [[Mystic Trial]])

## What it is
A weekly rally-boss event: your alliance rallies a giant bear (or set of bears) together. Rewards scale with the damage your rally does and how much you personally participate.

## How it works
- The alliance sets up rallies against the Bear during its active window.
- Whoever **leads** the rally has their [[Hero Gear Guide|Hero Gear]] and hero stats apply to the whole squad's damage — this is why your main march (top 3 heroes, geared) matters so much.
- Joiners contribute troops (based on hero level/XP), and get a share of rewards for participating, but their gear doesn't affect the rally's power.
- Rewards include unique upgrade materials that don't reliably drop anywhere else.

## [ONE]OneForAll Joiner Rules
Fill your **first hero slot** (the leftmost one) with one of these three, in priority order:

1. **[[Chenko]]** — girl with purple hair and a gun
2. **[[Yeonwoo]]** — girl with swords
3. **[[Amane]]** — girl with an umbrella

> [!important] Why the first slot matters
> Only the **first slot hero's** top skill has a chance to trigger as a bonus rally skill when you join. That's why we're strict about these three specifically — they're the heroes whose skills are worth that slot.

**Fill your other two hero slots with whatever heroes you have** — it doesn't matter as much once the first slot is locked in correctly.

**If you don't have any of these three:** that's completely fine — just send troops only, don't force a worse hero into the first slot.

- ❌ Don't use any hero besides Chenko / Yeonwoo / Amane in the first slot
- ✅ Troops-only joining is always acceptable if you don't have them
- ✅ Ask an officer if you're not sure which hero you have that qualifies

## Prep checklist (do this before the window opens)
- [ ] Confirm your **main march** (top 3 heroes) is geared and troops are healed/ready
- [ ] Make sure you're online and available for the full active window — rallies resolve almost instantly once launched, so late joiners lose most of the value
- [ ] Coordinate rally timing with your alliance in advance (this is where R4/R5 officers post reminders)
- [ ] Don't start training high-tier troops right before Bear Hunt — save that for a Training Event window instead

## Common mistakes
- Members joining rallies late and getting little to no reward
- Spreading Hero Gear investment across 5+ heroes instead of focusing gear on a tight main-3 rally squad
- Skipping it because "10 minutes isn't worth it" — it's one of the two highest-value events per minute spent in the whole game

## Teaching point for new members
> Bear Hunt = show up on time, join the rally the officers call, don't overthink it. If you're rally leading, your gear matters. If you're joining, just bring your best troops and show up fast.
