# Buccaneer Bounty

**Type:** Semi-regular event (roughly every 4–6 weeks, often 1–2 weeks after a new hero generation launches) — 7 days, chest-opening + shop
**Priority:** 🟠 High — one of the few sources of Widgets (hero exclusive gear), plus Truegold and Pet Materials once your kingdom is old enough for them

## What it is
A three-part event built around collecting **Corsair Keys**, opening chests for a shot at **Pearls of Enigma**, and spending those pearls in a special shop before it closes.

## The three components
1. **Harbor Drill** (+ Daily Drill) — complete daily tasks to earn Corsair Keys and Treasure Hunt Points. Treasure Hunt Points fill a milestone track that pays out more keys, speedups, hero EXP, and gems.
2. **Buccaneer Bounty** (the chests) — spend Corsair Keys here to open chests for random rewards, including Pearls of Enigma. There are also milestone rewards for total chests opened over the event.
3. **Emporium of Enigma** (the shop) — spend Pearls of Enigma on Widgets (current-generation hero exclusive gear only), a daily-refreshing 5× Forgehammer bundle, and — once your kingdom is old enough — Truegold or Pet Materials chests. This section runs one day longer than the other two so you have time to spend down your pearls.

> [!important] Keys are permanent, pearls are not
> **Corsair Keys carry over between cycles** — hoarding them across multiple Buccaneer Bounty events to save up for a specific milestone or shop item (like a skin) is a completely valid strategy. **Pearls of Enigma expire at the end of the event** and convert to basic resources — spend every pearl you have before the Emporium closes.

## Daily tasks (examples — check in-game each cycle, exact tasks vary)
- Daily login
- Use 1,000m of Speedups
- Defeat 10 Beasts
- Use Epic Recruitment 2 times

## F2P Notes
- Prioritize completing daily tasks for free Corsair Keys over buying key packs with real money.
- If you're not chasing a specific shop item this cycle, it's completely fine to bank your keys for a future cycle rather than opening chests right away — they don't expire.

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Check the Harbor Drill daily tasks as soon as the event opens
- [ ] Decide up front whether you're spending keys this cycle or banking them for a future one
- [ ] Don't let Pearls of Enigma sit unspent — the Emporium closes a day after the rest of the event
