# Champagne Fair

**Type:** Roughly monthly solo event
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium — good value, low coordination needed

## What it is
A roughly monthly event that occurs after [[All Out]] but before [[Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)]] in the broader cycle. Key-saving matters here — save keys/currency from earlier in the month specifically for this event rather than spending them piecemeal.

## Core Strategy
- **Save your keys** in the weeks leading up to Champagne Fair — this is the classic mistake new players make (spending keys immediately as they're earned instead of stockpiling)
- Since it's solo, there's no coordination requirement — just show up with your saved currency ready to spend

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Stop spending event keys/currency in the days leading up to Champagne Fair
- [ ] Check the shop/reward list once it opens before spending everything on the first item

## Teaching point for new members
> Champagne Fair is monthly and solo — the trick is saving your keys beforehand instead of spending them as you go, so you have a big stockpile ready when the Fair actually opens.
