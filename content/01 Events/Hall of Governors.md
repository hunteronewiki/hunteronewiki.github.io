# Hall of Governors (HoG)

**Type:** Rotating individual event
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium-High — the key timing event for [[Governor Gear Guide|Governor Gear]] spending

## What it is
An event centered around Governor-level progress that rewards spending your **Gilded Threads** and **Satin Threads** for Governor Gear during its active window.

## Key rule: Gilded Thread timing
> [!important]
> **Never spend Gilded Threads outside of a Hall of Governors event** unless you're one or two materials away from a critical tier breakpoint. Spending during HoG earns event points on top of the normal gear progress — spending outside HoG wastes that bonus value.

- **Satin Threads** are lower-value/more replaceable (steady free daily supply from the Alliance Store), so timing them around HoG matters less.
- If you're considering **buying** thread packs with real money, Gilded Thread packs are worth considering specifically when timed with an HoG window — Satin Thread packs usually aren't worth paying for since they're easy to get for free.

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Stockpile Gilded Threads between HoG windows rather than spending them piecemeal
- [ ] Check your Governor Gear tier breakpoints before HoG starts so you know exactly what to push
- [ ] Only break the "wait for HoG" rule if you're 1-2 materials from a breakpoint

## Common mistakes
- Spending Gilded Threads the moment you get them instead of saving for HoG
- Buying Gilded Thread packs outside of an HoG window

## Teaching point for new members
> Hall of Governors is when you spend your Gilded Threads on Governor Gear — save them up between events and dump them during HoG for bonus event points. Don't spend Gilded Threads outside this window unless you're one step from a tier breakpoint.
