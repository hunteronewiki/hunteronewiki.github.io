# Hall of Heroes

**Type:** Weekly solo event, Sunday–Tuesday (3 days)
**Priority:** 🟠 High — the only way to buy previous-generation Mythic hero shards **and** their Widgets after that generation has passed

## What it is
A weekly shop event where you spend **Mark of Valors** to unlock Mythic Heroes. Unlike [[Hero Roulette]] (which pulls from your *current* generation's pool with gems), Hall of Heroes specifically lets you go back and buy **any previous generation's** Mythic hero — shards and Widget both — that you missed the first time around.

## Mark of Valors
- You get **1 free** Mark of Valor each time the event runs, plus 100 free gems/day for the 3-day window (300 gems total)
- Marks otherwise cost **150 gems each**, capped at 99 purchasable with gems per event (the rest requires real-money packs)
- **100 Marks unlock one hero.** Using the 300 free gems knocks the effective gem cost down from 14,850 to about 14,550.
- Marks **never expire** — but they're **generation-locked**. A Gen 3 Mark only works in the Gen 3 section of the shop; it doesn't carry forward to Gen 4's shop once your kingdom advances. Since old generations' sections stick around in the shop, an unused old Mark isn't wasted, just locked to that generation's heroes.

> [!warning] Current generation is limited here
> For your kingdom's **current** generation, Hall of Heroes only offers **one of the three** heroes, and **no current-gen Widget** at all — [[Hero Roulette]] is still the main way to chase your current generation's heroes. Hall of Heroes earns its value on **past** generations you've since outgrown the normal acquisition window for.

## Shop packs
Five rarity tiers — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Mythic — priced $4.99–$99.99, each including Marks plus gems, VIP XP, and top-up credit. Packs reset daily during the 3-day window.

## F2P Notes
- Free Marks + free gems alone won't unlock a hero on their own — treat Hall of Heroes primarily as where you spend **leftover** gems you weren't already earmarking for [[Hero Roulette]] or [[Gem Spending Priority|other priorities]].
- If you're missing a specific older-generation Mythic hero (for a joiner role, a Widget, etc.), this is the only reliable place to still get them — worth checking every week even if you're not actively spending.

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Check which generation(s) you still have unspent, unlocked heroes for before the event opens
- [ ] Decide in advance whether this week's gems go here or toward [[Hero Roulette]] — don't double-spend on both without a plan
- [ ] If chasing a specific old-gen Widget, confirm the cost (roughly 1 Widget = 2 hero shards' worth of Marks) before committing gems
