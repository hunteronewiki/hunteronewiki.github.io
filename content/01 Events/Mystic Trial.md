# Mystic Trial

**Type:** Fixed weekly event, mostly solo/PvE with multiple "zones"
**Priority:** 🔴 Non-negotiable — pairs with [[Bear Hunt]] as the two must-do weekly events

## What it is
A rotating set of zone-based challenges. Different zones focus on different reward types:

| Zone | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Crystal Cave** | Governor Charm materials |
| **Forest of Life** | Pet-related progression |
| **Kingshot Coliseum** | Gear-focused rewards |
| **Knowledge Nexus** | Research-focused rewards |
| **Molten Fort** | Governor Gear materials |
| **Radiant Spire** | (verify current focus in-game) |

> [!tip] [ONE]OneForAll formation ratios
> See the table below for our alliance's exact troop ratio per zone.

Each zone has its own **stat rules and troop ratio expectations** for the "formation" you send in — check the specific zone's rules each time since they differ from your normal rally/garrison ratios.

## How it works
- You send in troops (formation matters — follow the zone's specific stat rules, not your standard rally ratio)
- You progress through stages; getting "stuck" at a stage usually means your troop ratio or stat allocation is mismatched for that zone
- A stage optimizer/calculator (many community sites have one) can help min-max your specific formation

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Check which zone is active this week before building your formation
- [ ] Don't reuse your Bear Hunt/rally ratio blindly — Mystic Trial zones have their own rules
- [ ] If stuck on a stage, adjust ratio rather than just throwing more troops at it

## Common mistakes
- Sending the same troop composition every week regardless of zone
- Ignoring this event because it's "just PvE" — it's one of the best unique-material sources in the game

## Teaching point for new members
> Mystic Trial changes which "zone" is active each week — each zone rewards a different system (charms, gear, pets, research). Check the zone before you build your formation, and don't panic if you get stuck on a stage — it usually just means your troop ratio needs adjusting for that specific zone.
