# Swordland Showdown

**Type:** Rotating alliance PvP event (map-control style)
**Priority:** 🟠 High — one of the biggest alliance events, big reward ceiling

## What it is
A cross-alliance battle on a special map full of capturable buildings. Your **alliance's** performance determines your bracket (Winner's/Loser's), and your **personal** performance determines your reward tier within that bracket.

> [!important] The most important mindset shift
> Winning isn't the only thing that matters. **It's often better to be a top scorer in the Loser's Bracket than a low scorer in the Winner's Bracket.** Always maximize your own Personal Relic Points regardless of how the overall match is going.

## No permanent troop losses
- All troop losses go to an infirmary with **unlimited capacity** and heal for free once you leave the battlefield.
- You must **enter with a clean infirmary** — heal any pre-existing wounded troops before the event starts.

## Scoring — two point types
- **Alliance Relic Points** → determines Winner's Bracket vs Loser's Bracket
- **Personal Relic Points** → determines your individual reward tier

### Ways to earn Relic Points
1. **First Control** of a building — big one-time bonus (both alliance + personal points)
2. **Ongoing Occupation** — steady point stream over time; **Swordshrine** and **Sanctums** are the most valuable buildings to hold
3. **Loot crates** — spawn when you capture a building from an enemy; boosts both point types
4. **Random resource caches** (Phase 3+) — check the minimap magnifying glass icon for exact locations
5. **Defeating enemy troops** — personal points only

## Phases & Key Buildings
- **Royal Stables** — 50% reduction in teleport cooldown (lets you reposition faster)
- **Bell Tower** — 50% reduction in time needed to capture buildings
- **Abbey / Sanctums** — high point value
- At the **15-minute mark**: Mercenary Camp, Hall of Reformation, and Swordland Shrine open.
  - **Swordland Shrine** — the single highest point-value building
  - **Hall of Reformation** — gives a 15% damage boost + damage reduction; very strong, worth holding
  - **Mercenary Camp** — usable to attack enemy-occupied buildings

## Suggested Alliance Roles
| Role | Who | Job |
|---|---|---|
| **Tank / Holder** | Strong defensive-hero players | Hold key outposts (Swordshrine, Sanctums), send reinforcements, lead defensive rallies |
| **Hunter** | Your alliance's strongest attackers | Actively hunt and wipe the enemy's strongest players to disrupt their point generation; use teleports for surprise attacks |
| **Support/Gatherer** | Everyone else | Capture lower-value buildings, gather from resource caches, keep pressure up |

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Heal your infirmary completely before the event starts
- [ ] Know your assigned role (Tank/Hunter/Support) ahead of time
- [ ] Prioritize Swordshrine and Sanctums over lower-value buildings
- [ ] Save teleports for surprise Hunter-role attacks late game

## Common mistakes
- Entering with wounded troops already in the infirmary (blocks entry)
- Chasing "winning" the bracket instead of maximizing personal score
- Ignoring the Hall of Reformation's buff — it's one of the strongest map bonuses

## Teaching point for new members
> Swordland Showdown = capture and hold buildings, don't worry about "losing" the match — just rack up your own points. No troop losses are permanent here, so play aggressively. Hold Swordshrine/Sanctums if you're a tank, hunt weak enemies if you're strong, or just capture whatever's open and gather from caches if you're newer.
