# Treasure Cove

**Type:** Rotating 5-day solo map-fragment event
**Priority:** Medium

## What it is
A 5-day event built around collecting map fragments to reveal a treasure map, with a free daily chest and an optional "Captain's Treasure" choice.

## Key Rules
- **Claim the free daily chest every day** — it's free value, don't miss a day
- **Choosing your Captain's Treasure** locks in a specific reward path for the event — think about which reward track you actually want before committing
- **Watch for claim traps** — some options look like better value but cost more than they return; compare pack costs to the reward directly before buying in

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Log in daily specifically to grab the free chest
- [ ] Decide your Captain's Treasure choice early rather than switching mid-event

## Teaching point for new members
> Treasure Cove runs 5 days — grab the free daily chest every single day without fail, and think carefully before locking in your Captain's Treasure choice.
