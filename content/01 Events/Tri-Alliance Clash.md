# Tri-Alliance Clash

**Type:** Monthly cross-kingdom alliance PvP event — one-hour battle, week-long build-up
**Priority:** 🟠 High — only comes around once a month, and a bad formation call costs the whole alliance the match

## What it is
Three alliances — often from different kingdoms — are matched together for a single, structured one-hour battle over map control. Whoever holds the most points at the end wins.

## Eligibility
- Alliance must rank in the **top 20** on the kingdom's power leaderboard
- A second legion requires the alliance to be Level 6+ with 40+ active members
- Each deployed legion needs at least 15 members to be matchmade
- Individual participants need **Town Center 16+** and no 5+ consecutive inactive days before the event

## Week-long schedule leading to the battle
- **Monday–Tuesday:** Voting
- **Wednesday–Thursday:** Sign-up
- **Friday:** Matchmaking
- **Saturday:** The battle itself, in your chosen time slot

## Battle phases (60 minutes total)
1. **Preparation** (3 min) — no captures yet
2. **Seize & Conquer** (17 min) — capture your own buildings and push into enemy territory for smaller buildings (Garrisons stay shielded)
3. **Garrison Occupation** (20 min) — Garrisons open up; they're worth roughly **3× a Cluster of Ruins** in points per minute, so they're the priority target once this phase opens
4. **Temple Onslaught** (20 min) — the Temple becomes capturable; whoever holds it at the final horn gets a massive point bonus on top of everything else

## Squad Setup
Bring **3 pairs of heroes** (9 total, split into 3 formations):
- Rank your heroes highest-to-lowest power, then split into 3 squads of 3 (top 3, next 3, next 3)
- Skip heroes with no battle skills (e.g. [[Diana]]) and skip Blue-rarity heroes — they don't pull their weight here even if they'd otherwise make your top 9

## Suggested Alliance Roles
| Role | Job |
|---|---|
| **Attack** | Push into enemy territory, contest Garrisons and the Temple directly |
| **Defender** | Hold your own captured buildings against counter-attacks |
| **Floater** | Reinforce whichever side (attack or defense) needs it as the match develops |

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Confirm your alliance is eligible (top 20 power, legion size requirements) before sign-up closes Thursday
- [ ] Rank heroes and lock in your 3 formations ahead of Saturday — don't improvise during the battle
- [ ] Assign Attack/Defender/Floater roles and have voice coordination ready before Preparation ends
- [ ] Plan to shift focus to Garrisons the moment Garrison Occupation opens, then to the Temple for Onslaught

## Common mistakes
- Chasing kills instead of point control — this event is won on buildings held, not enemies defeated
- Bringing Diana or Blue-rarity heroes into a formation just because their power score is high
- Waiting until Saturday to decide roles and formations instead of settling them during the week-long lead-in
