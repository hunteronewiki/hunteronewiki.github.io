# Viking Vengeance

**Type:** Rotating alliance rally event
**Priority:** 🟡 Medium — good rewards, needs coordination

## What it is
An alliance rally event against Viking-themed bosses/waves, with a difficulty selection and HQ wave mechanics.

## Core Strategy
- **Difficulty choice matters** — pick a difficulty your alliance can actually clear consistently rather than always maxing out; consistent clears beat occasional high-difficulty failures
- **Joiner hero setup** — bring joiner heroes that add troop count/support skills rather than heroes built purely for rally-leading
- **HQ wave strategy** — waves escalate, so plan reinforcement timing and don't let early waves overwhelm your garrison
- **Alliance reinforcement/coordination** — call rallies in advance so your alliance can mass on the same target

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Confirm difficulty level with officers before joining
- [ ] Set up your joiner hero lineup for support/troop count, not just gear
- [ ] Be ready for the HQ wave escalation — don't get caught undergeared on later waves

## Teaching point for new members
> Viking Vengeance is an alliance rally event — join the difficulty the officers pick (don't freelance into a harder one), bring good joiner heroes, and expect the waves to get tougher as it goes.
