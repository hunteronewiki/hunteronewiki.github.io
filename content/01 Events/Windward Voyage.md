# Windward Voyage

**Type:** Rotating solo chest/gacha-style event
**Priority:** Medium — decent F2P value if you plan around the milestone

## What it is
A voyage-based chest event: you send out voyages (roughly 8 hours each) and open chests using earned slots, working toward map fragments and a milestone reward.

## Key Rules
- **Never open singles** — always wait until you have enough for a bulk/batch open rather than opening one chest at a time, since batch opens are more efficient
- **Time your compass/voyage launches** so they complete when you're actually online to claim and relaunch — a voyage sitting finished and unclaimed wastes potential launches
- **F2P milestone target:** commonly cited around 20 voyages completed as a reasonable free-to-play goal for this event's cycle

## Prep checklist
- [ ] Launch voyages as soon as slots free up rather than letting them sit idle
- [ ] Save chests to open in batches, not one at a time
- [ ] Track your progress toward the ~20-voyage F2P milestone

## Teaching point for new members
> Windward Voyage is about keeping your voyage slots always working — relaunch as soon as one finishes, and never open chests one at a time, always batch them.
