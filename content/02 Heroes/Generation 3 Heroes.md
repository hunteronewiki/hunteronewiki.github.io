# Generation 3 Heroes

Unlocks around **Day 120** (server age > ~120 days). This is where Kingshot's most iconic meta-defining heroes appear — priorities shift dramatically once your server hits this window.

## [[Petra]] — Cavalry
- **Kit:** Relies on tarot-card style random effects. Her "Change of Fate" skill can deal up to 600% damage on a lucky proc; "Turn Card" can also provide healing.
- **Role:** Best F2P cavalry pick for multiple generations running — a long-term investment that doesn't fall off quickly. Strong rally-leader material.

## [[Eric]] — Infantry
- **Kit:** A tank/warrior hybrid — boosts his own defense when attacked, has a chance to stun enemies, deals modest damage.
- **Role:** Garrison-focused; one of the best defender-widget heroes available at this point in the game (alongside Jabel, Zoe, Jaeger, Alcar, Margot).

## [[Jaeger]] — (role/troop type per your server's roster — check in-game skill kit)
- **Note:** Some tier-list sources note Jaeger's **raw stats** beat Marlin's when Marlin's widget isn't active, making Jaeger a solid alternative core hero once available.

## F2P Priority Order (Gen 3 window)
1. **Petra** — top cavalry priority, long-term investment
2. **Eric** — garrison core alongside Zoe
3. Continue holding **Zoe** and **Marlin** from Gen 2 — they still perform well here, don't discard them prematurely
4. **Jaeger** if you need a stat-focused alternative to Marlin

## Key transition reminder
> When your server crosses into Gen 3, don't rush to fully replace a maxed Gen 1/2 hero with a fresh Gen 3 pick at low stars. A 5-star older hero generally still outperforms a 3-star new one — level the replacement up first, then swap your gear over.
