# Generation 4 Heroes

Unlocks around **Day 200** (server age > ~200 days).

## [[Rosa]] — Archer
- **Kit:** Debuff immunity skill that completely shuts down stun-heavy enemy teams.
- **Role:** A-tier archer leader and the **Arena empress** — build her without hesitation if you spend meaningful time in Arena. Her impact drops somewhat in large rallies, where she's a solid alternative to (not a full replacement for) Marlin or Vivian.

## [[Alcar]] — (Infantry/Defender profile)
- **Role:** One of the defender-widget heroes worth holding for garrison duty alongside Zoe, Eric, and Jaeger.

## [[Margot]] — Cavalry
- **Kit:** First Expedition skill is a **non-chance-based** offensive buff — meaning it stacks reliably as a joiner buff, unlike chance-based skills.
- **Role:** The hidden gem for Bear Hunt — combine her with Chenko and Amadeus for very high joiner-stacked bear damage. Also a solid garrison cavalry pick thanks to her defender widget.

## Rally/Formation Note for Gen 4+
By Generation 4, the commonly recommended rally troop ratio shifts to roughly **1/10/89** (Infantry/Cavalry/Archer) for offensive rallies — a heavy archer-weighted composition. Defense/garrison ratios stay closer to **60/15/25**.

## F2P Priority Order (Gen 4 window)
1. **Rosa** if you're Arena-focused
2. **Margot** for Bear Hunt joiner stacking (pairs with Chenko + Amadeus)
3. **Alcar** for garrison rounding out your defender core
4. Continue holding Petra/Eric/Zoe as your rally and garrison cores unless directly outclassed
