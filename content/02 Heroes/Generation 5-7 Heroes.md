# Generation 5–7 Heroes

Late-kingdom rosters. Newer heroes generally carry ~20% higher base stats than the prior generation, so expect a real power spike each time — but that doesn't mean immediately discarding older heroes (see the transition note at the bottom).

## Generation 5 (unlocks ~Day 280)
- **[[Vivian]]** — described as "the ultimate force multiplier." Her signature skill ("Crouching Tiger") makes the **entire enemy army** take 25% more damage from all your squads — a global amplification effect that stacks with everything else. Pair her with a strong cavalry/archer damage dealer (Yang or Ava once available) to maximize the payoff.
- **[[Long Fei]]** — part of the Gen 5 core; check current role/skill data in-game as this hero's exact kit info was less consistently reported across sources.
- **[[Thrud]]** — takes over as the cavalry rally leader role from Petra once available.

## Generation 6 (release timing varies by server)
- **[[Sophia]]**
- **[[Triton]]**
- **[[Yang]]** — pairs well with Vivian's global damage amplification as a rally-lane core.

## Generation 7 (released May 25, 2026 on Kingdoms #1–25; unlocks around Day 440-449+)
- **[[Ava]]** — Gen 7 cavalry addition; slots into rally cores once your server hits the Day 449+ window.
- **[[Charles]]** — commonly recommended as your Gen 7 garrison anchor (add him alongside Zoe/Eric/Alcar).
- **[[Wee and Woo|Wee & Woo]]** — slots into Bear Hunt joiner stacks (alongside Chenko + Amane + Margot) once available.

## Suggested Rally/Garrison Cores at Gen 7 (adjust to your server's actual generation)
- **Rally core across lanes:** Petra + Vivian + Yang + Ava
- **Garrison core:** Zoe + Eric + Alcar (+ Charles once unlocked)
- **Bear Hunt joiner stack:** Chenko + Amane + Margot (+ Wee & Woo once unlocked)

## Important transition reminder
> Older heroes lose raw combat stat competitiveness in Conquest as new generations arrive, but their **Expedition skill buffs often stay relevant far longer**. Don't necessarily keep pouring stars/shards into them once outclassed — but keep them slotted in Expedition and Bear Hunt lineups until a direct, confirmed upgrade is available. A few heroes (Amadeus, Helga, Marlin) are widely considered exceptions that remain S+ tier because their Expedition buffs are irreplaceable — no newer hero replicates what they bring to team-wide damage/survival.
