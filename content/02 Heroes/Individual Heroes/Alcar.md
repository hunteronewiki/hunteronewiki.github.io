## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net and related trackers); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Glorious Mark** — Enters an invulnerable state (unable to move or cast skills, immune to control effects) and reduces Damage Taken by 70% for nearby friendly squads for 2s.
- **Boneshatter** — Spears a line of enemies, dealing Attack × 98% damage and increasing the Damage Taken for enemies hit by 20% for 2s.
- **Second Wind** — While Glorious Mark is active, restores Attack × 42% Health every second for 5s.

**Expedition**
- **Rescuing Hands** — Reduces Infantry and Archer's Damage Taken by 70% every 5 turns for 2 turns.
- **Praetorian Will** — Increases Infantry Damage Dealt by 100%, with a smaller bonus for Cavalry and Archers.
- **Carpe Diem** — Increases Infantry Damage Dealt per attack, and increases Damage Taken by the target for one turn.


## Role
One of the defender-widget heroes worth holding for garrison duty, alongside [[Zoe]], [[Eric]], and [[Jaeger]]. Rounds out a strong garrison core through the mid-game.
