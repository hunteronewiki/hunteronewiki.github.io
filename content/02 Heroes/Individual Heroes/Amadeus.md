# Amadeus
**Generation:** 1
**Troop Type:** Infantry
**How to get:** Typically requires [[VIP System|VIP]] 7+ or a paid acquisition path — not freely available.

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Combo Slash** — Three powerful slashes, each dealing Attack × 224% damage.
- **Arcane Swordship** — A burst of arcane energy with each sword slash, dealing Attack × 23% damage to frontal foes.
- **Onslaught** — Increasing Attack by 48% when Health is below 50%.

**Expedition**
- **Battle Ready** — Increasing the total Squads' Lethality by 25%.
- **Way of the Blade** — Increasing the total Squads' Attack by 25%.
- **Unrighteous Strike** — 40% chance of increasing damage dealt by 50% for all squads.


## Kit
His widget ("Aegis of Fate") multiplies Rally Attack. His first Expedition skill gives a **flat, unconditional** Lethality buff — no proc chance attached.

## Role
S-tier rally leader **and** one of the best Bear Hunt/[[Bear Hunt|Bear Trap]] joiners in the game, because the buff isn't chance-based — it always applies. His kit stays relevant across many generations longer than almost any other hero.

## F2P Notes
If he's not accessible to you, don't chase him — redirect those resources into [[Zoe]] instead.
