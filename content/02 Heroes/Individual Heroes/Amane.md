## Kit
Her first Expedition skill provides +25% team Lethality (same category as [[Yeonwoo]]'s). When paired with [[Chenko]] in the same rally, their combined stacking effect reaches a **1.56x multiplier** — stronger than running two Chenkos together (1.50x).

## Role
Part of the strongest known Bear Hunt joiner-stacking trio alongside Chenko and Yeonwoo. Also useful in later generations as a joiner alongside [[Margot]] and Wee & Woo once unlocked.
