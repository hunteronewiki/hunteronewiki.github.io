# Chenko
**Generation:** 2 (Epic rarity)
**Troop Type:** Cavalry
**Appearance:** Purple hair, carries a gun
**How to get:** Easy — via events, no gems required.

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Burst Fire** — A rapid barrage, dealing Attack × 75% damage to enemies in front every 0.5s for 2s.
- **Defense Upgrade** — Upgrades her armor, increasing Defense by 70%.
- **Weapon Upgrade** — Upgraded weapon boosts Attack by 24%.

**Expedition**
- **Stand of Arms** — Increasing the total Squads' Lethality by 25%.
- **Shield Wall** — Reduces damage taken by 20% for all troops.


## Kit
First Expedition skill gives a **flat +25% team Lethality** — no conditions attached. Only has 3 skills total, and limited Conquest performance.

## Role
The **undisputed F2P queen of Bear Hunt joiners.** When multiple Chenkos join the same rally, the effect can stack, making her one of the single best sources of Bear Hunt damage in the game.

> [!important] [ONE]OneForAll's #1 required Bear Trap joiner
> Chenko is the **top pick** for the first hero slot when joining a Bear Trap rally in our alliance. See [[Bear Hunt]] for the full rule.

## Investment Guidance
Raise her to **4 stars** to unlock her full skill set, then **stop investing further** — she's not worth building as a main combat hero.
