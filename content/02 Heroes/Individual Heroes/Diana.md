## Role
Frequently used as a benchmark for "don't bother" comparisons — e.g., guides note that even a 3-star [[Marlin]] outperforms a 5-star Diana. Rare-rarity heroes like Diana generally lack meaningful battle skills; awaken for passive/economic buffs only, don't invest combat resources.

Despite the weak combat skills, she has one genuinely useful utility: her first Expedition skill reduces stamina usage by 10–20%, which makes her worth bringing along specifically for stamina-heavy grinding like [[Cesares Fury]] — this is a utility pick, not a combat one, so it doesn't contradict the "don't invest in her" guidance above.
