## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Ground Shock** — Dealing Attack × 168% damage to the target ahead while reducing targets' Attack Speed by 50% for 4s.
- **Veterancy** — Attacks against Eric have a 16% chance of increasing his Defense by 20% for 2s (max 5 stacks).
- **Hammer of Justice** — Dealing Attack × 112% damage in a fan-shaped arc with a 30% chance of stunning targets for 1s.

**Expedition**
- **Holy Warrior** — Reducing the total Enemy Squads' Attack by 20%.
- **Conviction** — Reducing Squads' Damage Taken by 20%.
- **Exhortation** — Increasing the total Squads' Health by 25%.


## Kit
A tank/warrior hybrid — boosts his own Defense when attacked, has a chance to stun enemies, deals modest damage.

## Role
Garrison-focused. One of the best defender-widget heroes available at this point (alongside [[Jabel]], [[Zoe]], [[Jaeger]], [[Alcar]], [[Margot]]). Anchors garrison cores well into later generations alongside Zoe and Alcar.
