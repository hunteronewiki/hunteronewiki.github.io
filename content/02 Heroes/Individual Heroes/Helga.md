## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Antler Assault** — Performs an antler charge, dealing Attack × 224% area of effect damage, knocking targets back, and stunning them for 1s.
- **Spear Charge** — Deals Attack × 210% damage to the target.
- **Fury** — When taking damage, 10% chance of increasing Attack by 12% for 3s. Stacks up to 5 times.

**Expedition**
- **Oath of Guardian** — 40% chance of reducing damage taken by 50% for all squads.
- **Echoes of Valhalla** — Increases the total Squads' Attack by 25%.
- **Nature's Balance** — Increases the total Squads' Lethality by 25%.
