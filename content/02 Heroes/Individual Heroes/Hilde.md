**Troop Type:** Cavalry

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Rejuvenating Plea** — Immediately restores Health equal to her Attack × 280% to all friendly heroes.
- **Redeemer** — Immediately restores Health equal to her Attack × 140% to the weakest friendly hero.
- **Intimidation** — Disables the enemy target for 1s while dealing Attack × 196% damage.

**Expedition**
- **Noble Path** — Increases the total squads' Attack by 15% and Defense by 10%.
- **Elixir of Strength** — Grants all squads' attacks a 25% chance of dealing 200% damage.
- **Trial by Fire** — 40% chance of reducing damage taken by 50% for all squads.


## Kit
A dedicated healer who can also briefly disable/stun enemies.

## Role
One of the best healers in the game. Less flashy if you don't specifically need healing, but essential when you do. Also has strong joiner-stacking value — pairs well with [[Saul]] from Gen 1 thanks to their differing underlying skill IDs (their combined multiplier is stronger than a typical hero pairing).
