# Jabel
**Generation:** 1
**Troop Type:** Cavalry
**How to get:** Free — automatic Day 2 login reward, or via Hero Hall recruitment. Nearly every player has her within the first week.

## Base Stats
_Conquest stats at 6★, sourced from community data — see the note below the widget._

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Gallant Throw** — Throws a spear that pierces defenses, dealing Attack × 252% area of effect damage and pinning the targets for 1.5s.
- **Brave the Storm** — A true knight bravely confronts enemies, dealing Attack × 210% damage to the target.
- **Persistence** — Adversity ignites endless potential, increasing Attack Speed by 60% when Health is below 50%.

**Expedition**
- **Rally Flag** — Has a 40% chance of reducing damage taken by 50% for all squads.
- **Hero's Domain** — Grants all squads a 50% chance of dealing 50% more damage when attacking.
- **Youthful Rage** — Increases damage dealt by 25% for all squads.


## Kit
AoE damage skill plus a squad-wide damage reduction buff active during Expedition.

## Role
Garrison/defender anchor — not a rally leader. Her defender widget keeps her useful as a city-holder well into Generation 3, longer than most Gen 1 heroes.

## F2P Notes
Zero gem cost. Build her immediately — one of the best "free foundation" heroes in the game.
