## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net and related trackers); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Ambush** — AoE bomb attack, scaling up to Attack × 420% damage.
- **Parry** — Passive ability granting up to 25% chance to dodge incoming attacks, avoiding all damage.
- **Stiletto** — Deals up to Attack × 140% damage and immobilizes the target for 1.5s.

**Expedition**
- **Warbringer** — Increases total Squads' Attack by 25%.
- **Subterfuge** — Grants all Squads a chance to dodge Normal Attacks (up to 20%).
- **Sleight Hand** — Gives Margot's Cavalry a 40% chance to perform an extra attack.


## Kit
First Expedition skill is a **non-chance-based** offensive buff — meaning it stacks reliably as a joiner buff, unlike chance-based skills.

## Role
The hidden gem for Bear Hunt — combine her with [[Chenko]] and [[Amadeus]] for very high joiner-stacked bear damage. Also a solid garrison cavalry pick thanks to her defender widget. Continues to slot into the joiner stack alongside Wee & Woo once Gen 7 unlocks.
