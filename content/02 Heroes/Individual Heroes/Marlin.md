# Marlin
**Generation:** 2 (unlocks ~Day 50)
**Troop Type:** Archer
**How to get:** Hall of Heroes — unlock with 10 shards, then level to 3–4 stars using generation Mythic Shards. (Not a Hero Roulette feature hero — that's [[Zoe]] for Gen 2.)

- **Fermented Blow** — Deadly airborne concoction deals Attack × 70% area of effect damage.

**Expedition**
- **Wild Card** — 40% chance of increasing damage dealt by 50% for all squads.
- **Rumhead** — 20% chance of reducing the total Enemy Squads' Lethality by 50% for 2 turns when attacking.
- **Dynamo** — 50% chance of increasing damage dealt by 50%.


## Kit
AoE damage dealer who can also stun. Has three stacking effect types:
- **Wild Card** — 40% chance to increase damage output by 50%
- **Rumhead** — 20% chance to reduce enemy Lethality by 50%
- **Dynamo** — 50% chance to deal 50% extra damage

## Role
S+ tier rally-attack leader even against newer-generation heroes, purely because of this team-wide damage support kit. Also strong in Arena and Expedition — his offensive Widget ("Mistweaver") gives him excellent long-term value.

## F2P Notes
Even a 3-star Marlin outperforms a 5-star [[Diana]] — the shard investment pays off fast. Your ranged carry all the way into Gen 3.
