# Petra
**Generation:** 3 (unlocks ~Day 120)
**Troop Type:** Cavalry
**How to get:** Hero Roulette (Gen 3 feature).

## Base Stats
_Conquest stats at 6★, sourced from community data — see the note below the widget._

| Attack | 3330 |
| Defense | 3330 |
| Health | 33300 |

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Dichotomy** — Dealing Attack × 378% damage and inflicting one of: reducing the target's Attack by 20% for 2s, or stunning for 1.5s.
- **Change of Fate** — Floating damage based on Attack × 70% — a random amount between 5% and 600% of that base value.
- **Turn Card** — Heals the lowest-Health friendly hero based on Attack × 140% — a random amount between 5% and 400% of that base value.

**Expedition**
- **Evil Eye** — 50% chance of increasing the target's damage taken by 50%.
- **The Favor** — 50% chance of increasing the Squads' Attack by 50%.
- **The Shield** — 40% chance of reducing damage taken by 50% for all squads.


## Kit
Relies on tarot-card style random effects. "Change of Fate" can deal up to 600% damage on a lucky proc; "Turn Card" can also provide healing.

## Role
Best F2P cavalry pick for multiple generations running — doesn't fall off quickly. Strong rally-leader material and part of the long-term rally core (Petra + [[Vivian]] + [[Yang]] + [[Ava]] at Gen 7).

## Exclusive Gear
**Fate's Writ**

- **Conquest:** Attack 630, Defense 630, Health 6300
- **Escort:** Attack 210, Defense 210, Health 2100
- **Expedition:** Lethality 70%, Health 70%

- **Weighted Deck** — Increasing the upper and lower limit of her fluctuating skills by 150%.
- **Cosmic Eye** — Increasing Rally Squad Attack by 15%.
