## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net and related trackers); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Battle Focus** — Clears all debuffs (control effects, etc.) for all squads and increases their Attack for 5s, during which they're immune to debuffs.
- **Flutterfang** — Rosa spins her fan blade, dealing Attack × 300% damage to enemies in its path.
- **Swoon Chakra** — Reduces Attack Speed and healing effects by up to 60% for all enemy squads.

**Expedition**
- **Chaos Gambit** — 40% chance of increasing all Squads' Damage Dealt by 50%.
- **Rose of War** — Reduces damage dealt by 20% for all enemy squads.
- **Golden Rhythm** — Increases Archers' total Attack by 30%.


## Kit
Debuff immunity skill that completely shuts down stun-heavy enemy teams.

## Role
The **Arena empress** — build her without hesitation if you spend meaningful time in Arena. A-tier archer leader in large rallies too, though her impact there is somewhat lower than [[Marlin]] or [[Vivian]] — a strong alternative rather than a full replacement.
