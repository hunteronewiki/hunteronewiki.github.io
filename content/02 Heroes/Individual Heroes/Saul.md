# Saul
**Generation:** 1
**Troop Type:** Archer
**How to get:** Early — via the $1 starter pack or Hero Roulette.

## Base Stats
_Conquest stats at 6★, sourced from community data — see the note below the widget._

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Attack | 2697 |
| Defense | 2220 |
| Health | 16650 |

## Kit
Stuns enemies for 2 seconds, boosts his own attack speed, and gains Defense when his HP drops low. Also has a growth skill boosting Construction Speed.

## Role
His hidden value is in **joiner stacking** — his skill uses a different underlying skill ID than [[Hilde]]'s, so pairing Saul + Hilde produces a stronger combined multiplier than a typical hero pairing. Worth keeping for stackable joiner builds even once his raw combat stats fall behind.

## F2P Notes
Don't over-invest combat resources — his construction-speed skill has ongoing value even after his stats age out.
