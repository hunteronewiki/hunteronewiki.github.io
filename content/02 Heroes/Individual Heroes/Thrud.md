## Skills

**Conquest**

- **Brutal Throw** — Throws a spinning axe dealing Attack × 70% damage and stuns enemies for 1.5s.

**Expedition**
- **Battle Hunger** — Infantry and Archers take 15% less damage and deal 15% more.
- **Reckless Charge** — Cavalry has a 20% chance to deal 100% extra damage.
- **Ancestral Guidance** — Every four Cavalry attacks, increases all squads' damage dealt by 25%.
