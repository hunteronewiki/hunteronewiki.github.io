## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net and related trackers); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Gilded Barrage** — A coin bullet targeting heroes first, dealing Attack × 252% area damage and slowing enemy Attack Speed by 50% for 2s.
- **Lucky Strike** — Deals Attack × 140% damage with a 50% chance of dealing double damage.
- **Priceless Shot** — Creates a bullet barrage dealing 49% damage per second in an area for 3s.

**Expedition**
- **Crouching Tiger** — Permanently increases all enemy damage taken by 25% (all squads).
- **Focus Fire** — After every four attacks, all squads deal 100% extra damage, and the target takes 15% extra damage on its next hit.
- **Trap of Greed** — Equips Archers with coin traps, dealing 60% extra damage to all enemies on every 4th attack.


## Kit
Signature skill "Crouching Tiger" makes the **entire enemy army** take 25% more damage from all your squads — a global amplification effect that stacks with everything else.

## Role
Described as "the ultimate force multiplier." Pair her with a strong cavalry/archer damage dealer ([[Yang]] or [[Ava]] once available) to maximize the payoff. Core piece of the late-game rally lineup: [[Petra]] + Vivian + Yang + Ava.
