## Kit
Offers both rally joiner value and base development bonuses — a high-value Epic-rarity choice in the early game. Her first Expedition skill provides **+25% team Lethality**, using a different underlying effect type than [[Chenko]]'s — meaning the two effects can **stack together** rather than overwriting each other.

## Role
A top-tier Bear Hunt joiner alongside Chenko and [[Amane]]. Raise to 4 stars, use mainly as a rally joiner — Conquest and Arena performance is average, so heavy further investment isn't recommended.
