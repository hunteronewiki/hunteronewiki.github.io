# Zoe
**Generation:** 2 (unlocks ~Day 50)
**Troop Type:** Infantry
**How to get:** Hero Roulette — she's the dedicated feature banner for Gen 2.

## Skills
_Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level._

**Conquest**
- **Shield Strike** — Dealing Attack × 84% damage every 0.5s and amplifying damage taken by the target by 30% for 2s.
- **Agony Rush** — Immediately regains 40% of max Health under 50% Health. Can only activate once per battle.
- **Holy Might** — Boosts your heroes' Attack Speed by 7%.

**Expedition**
- **Sundering Wound** — Squads' attacks gain a 20% chance of inflicting Sunder, dealing 40% damage per turn for 3 turns.
- **Charisma** — Increases the total Squads' Attack by 25%.
- **Infinite Arsenal** — Squads' attacks gain a 50% chance of amplifying enemy squads' damage taken by 50%.


## Kit
Gets progressively stronger below 50% HP — a high-risk, high-reward tank/defensive profile.

## Role
**Your garrison cornerstone for 4+ generations.** Widely considered the single best F2P Infantry investment in the early-to-mid game. Anchors garrison cores well into Gen 7 (paired with [[Eric]], [[Alcar]], and [[Charles]]).

## F2P Notes
Top priority the moment Gen 2 unlocks — max her stars first, before anything else.
