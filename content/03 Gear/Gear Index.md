# Gear Index

> [!info] Scope: Mythic and Red tier only
> Grey/Green/Blue/Purple gear is intentionally left out of this index — by the time gear
> matters strategically, you should already be working in Mythic (Gold) and pushing toward Red.
> See [[Hero Gear Guide]] for the full upgrade path and materials.

Hero Gear has 12 pieces total: **Infantry, Cavalry, and Archer**, each with a **Helmet, Boots,
Chest, and Gloves**. Helmet and Boots grant **Lethality** for that troop type; Chest and Gloves
grant **Health**. Every piece follows the same enhancement curve — this index shows the
Mythic-tier range confirmed from community data.

## Mythic Tier Scaling (Level 0 → 100)

| Piece | Troop Type | Stat | At +0 | At +100 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Helmet | Infantry | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Boots | Infantry | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Chest | Infantry | Health | 15% | 50% |
| Gloves | Infantry | Health | 15% | 50% |
| Helmet | Cavalry | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Boots | Cavalry | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Chest | Cavalry | Health | 15% | 50% |
| Gloves | Cavalry | Health | 15% | 50% |
| Helmet | Archer | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Boots | Archer | Lethality | 15% | 50% |
| Chest | Archer | Health | 15% | 50% |
| Gloves | Archer | Health | 15% | 50% |

> [!warning] Red tier (level 101–200) isn't in the simulator yet
> Mythic gear converts to Red at level 100 (requires Level 100, Mastery Forging 10, 2 spare
> Mythic pieces of the same slot, and Town Center 30). Red then continues upgrading toward
> level 200. I couldn't independently verify the exact 101→200 scaling curve from a source
> I'd trust enough to put in front of the alliance, so the simulator below stops at +100
> rather than guessing. If someone finds a verified Red curve, this is easy to extend.

## Priority Order
Per [[Governor Gear Guide|the alliance's stated priority]] and general community consensus:
**Infantry Health (Chest/Gloves) → Archer Lethality (Helmet/Boots) → Cavalry Lethality
(Helmet/Boots) → Infantry Lethality → Archer Health → Cavalry Health.** Don't spread evenly —
hyper-focus your main march's 3 heroes' gear before touching anyone else's.
