# Hero Gear Guide

**Scope:** Gear equipped **on your heroes** — powers the rally leader's march. This is separate from [[Governor Gear Guide|Governor Gear]] (army-wide bonuses) — see that note for the parallel system.

> [!important] The single most important rule
> Hero Gear only applies when your hero is **leading** a rally. It does nothing when you join someone else's rally. See [[Hero Overview and Roles]] for the full breakdown of why your "main march" (top 3 heroes) is the only thing worth gearing.

## Unlock & Scaling
- Unlocks at **Town Center Level 15**.
- Gear progresses through **6 rarities**, each with a higher max level cap.
- A full Mythic set on your main march can represent tens of thousands of troop power — this is one of the single biggest power multipliers in the game.

## Upgrade Priority Order
**Exact sequence to follow at every milestone:**

**Infantry Gloves → Archer Helmet → Cavalry Helmet**

Advance each piece to your current milestone level before moving to the next piece — **never spread resources evenly across all pieces at once.** Focus over breadth.

## The Full Path: Mythic → Red

1. **Farm grey/green/blue/purple pieces** and merge them up
2. **Push Mythic pieces to Level 80–100** using scrolls and merges
3. **Mastery Forging unlocks at Mythic Level 20+** — this opens up specialized stat growth
4. **Red Gear imbuement** requires (verified as of ~April 2026):
   - Target piece at **Level 100**
   - **Mastery Forging 10**
   - **2 additional Mythic pieces** to sacrifice
   - **Town Center 30** with roughly 40 days of kingdom age
5. Once converted to **Red**, the piece can no longer be freely leveled — each **20-level checkpoint (120, 140, 160, 180, 200)** requires **Mithril** to unlock
6. Full Level 100 → 200 path costs roughly **150 Mithril per piece** — budget before starting a Red conversion (verify exact costs in your in-game imbuement screen)

## Materials & Sources
| Material | Best Sources |
|---|---|
| **Mithril** | Events and shops — gating resource for Red gear past Level 100 |
| **Forgehammers** | Enhancement/reforge, salvaging spare gear |
| **Gear pieces (F2P)** | Arena Shop Chests — Custom Hero Gear chests for specific pieces |
| **Gear pieces (gems)** | Hero Roulette targeted spins |
| **Gear pieces (events)** | [[Bear Hunt]], [[Swordland Showdown]], lucky chests (~1% drop rate — don't rely on these) |

## Reforge — recovering value from mistakes
Salvage/reforge extra or misplaced gear for **100% XP + hammer recovery**. If you geared a hero outside your main 3 before realizing the mistake, reforge that gear rather than leaving it stuck — it converts cleanly into Enhancement XP and Forgehammers you can redirect to your actual main march.

## Hero Gear vs Rally Role Reminder
- Applies **only** to the rally leader's march — never to joiners
- A hero's **skill** (not gear) is what matters for joiners — e.g., Chenko's flat Lethality buff is valuable purely from her kit, with zero gear investment needed
- Upgrading a rally-support skill (like an aura effect) lets a hero contribute meaningfully even in a "top 4 support" slot without full gear investment

## Common Mistakes
- Balancing resources across all gear pieces at once instead of following the priority sequence
- Gearing up 5 heroes instead of focusing your main 3
- Spending Mithril on non-priority pieces
- Forgetting to reforge gear stuck on non-main-march heroes
