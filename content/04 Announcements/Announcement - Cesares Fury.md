# Announcement: Cesares Fury

```
CESARES FURY

Pick your difficulty carefully — you only get ONE per event and can't switch once you start.

RALLY, DON'T SOLO, at levels 10, 20, 30, 40, and 50. These hit way harder than the levels around them. Ask in chat the second you hit one of these.

Scouting a Rebel costs stamina and gives you 1 hour to kill it before the scout expires — don't scout if you're not ready to fight right away.
```
