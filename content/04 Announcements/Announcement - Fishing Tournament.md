# Announcement: Fishing Tournament

```
FISHING TOURNAMENT

Casual, solo. Cast your line, dodge obstacles, go for depth. Deeper means rarer fish and more points.

Upgrade your Line before your Hook, no point in extra catch capacity if you can't reach the good depth yet.

Attempts regenerate every few hours. Spread them out, don't burn them all at once. Save boost items for when you're actually pushing for leaderboard rank.
```
