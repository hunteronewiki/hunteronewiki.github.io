# Announcement: Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)

```
KVK PREP WEEK

Biggest event in the game. Prep matters as much as the fight.

Stop spending gems on anything except gear and troops starting now. Queue up any building, research, or training you can before the window opens.

During the fight, follow officer callouts for Castle and turret control. Don't just chase random kills.

Losses in KvK are real, not auto-healed like Swordland Showdown. Don't overextend without a healing plan.
```
