# Announcement: Swordland Showdown

```
SWORDLAND SHOWDOWN

Heal your troops before this starts. You can't enter with wounded troops sitting in your infirmary.

We capture and hold buildings. No troops are lost permanently here, so play aggressive.

Tanks: hold Swordshrine and the Sanctums.
Hunters: go after the enemy's strongest players.
Everyone else: capture open buildings, grab loot crates.

Winning the match isn't the only thing that matters. Your own score decides your own reward, so keep playing for points even if we're not winning overall.
```
