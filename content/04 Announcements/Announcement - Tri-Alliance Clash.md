# Announcement: Tri-Alliance Clash

```
TRI-ALLIANCE CLASH

This is a monthly 1-hour battle against 2 other alliances. Only comes around once a month, so don't skip prep.

Rank your heroes and lock in your 3 formations (top 3 / next 3 / next 3) before Saturday. Skip Diana and any Blue-rarity heroes — they don't perform here even if their power looks good.

Once Garrison Occupation opens, prioritize Garrisons — they're worth about 3x a normal building in points per minute.

Whoever holds the Temple at the very end gets a massive bonus, so save strength for Temple Onslaught in the last 20 minutes.

Roles: Attack, Defender, Floater. Officers will assign these ahead of time — know yours before the battle starts.
```
