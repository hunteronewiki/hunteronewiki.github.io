# About

## About This Site
This wiki is built and maintained by **Hunter**, a member of **[ONE]OneForAll** in **Kingdom 2205**.
It started as an alliance knowledge base, but it's meant to work as a Wikipedia-style reference
for everything Kingshot — game mechanics, heroes, events, and strategy — not just for our
alliance, but as a general resource for anyone who plays.
