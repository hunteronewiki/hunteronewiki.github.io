# Common Confusions FAQ

Quick answers to the questions that come up most in alliance chat, each linking to the full page
for detail. Good page to pin alongside [[Kingshot Glossary]].

## What's the difference between Hero Gear, Governor Gear, and Governor Charms?
Three separate systems, three separate material tracks — you're never choosing between them,
just progressing all three in parallel:
- **[[Hero Gear Guide|Hero Gear]]** — equipped on individual heroes, only counts when that hero is your **rally leader**
- **[[Governor Gear Guide|Governor Gear]]** — equipped on you directly, boosts Attack/Defense for marches **you** lead
- **[[Governor Charms Guide|Governor Charms]]** — unlocked at Town Center 25, boosts Health/Lethality, doesn't touch Attack/Defense at all

## Why doesn't my gear do anything when I join someone else's rally?
Hero Gear and Governor Gear only apply when **you're** the rally leader. As a joiner, only your
troop count (based on hero level/XP) and your first-slot hero's first Expedition skill matter —
see [[Hero Overview and Roles]].

## My best Bear Hunt heroes — are they also my best Arena team?
Not necessarily. [[Arena and Conquest|Arena/Conquest]] adds together all 5 heroes' power and
scores off a different skill line (Conquest skills, not Expedition skills) than rally leading
does. Check each hero's Conquest skill separately before assuming your rally main-3 carries over.

## What's Truegold / TG8, and when do I get it?
High-tier troop levels gated by kingdom age rather than anything you can rush — Truegold unlocks
around day 220+, Tempered Truegold (TG8) around day 310+. See [[Kingshot Glossary]].

## Should I spend gems the moment I get them?
Almost never — see [[Gem Spending Priority]]. Nearly every gem-efficiency mistake in this game
comes down to spending outside the matching event window instead of holding for it.

## I'm new and overwhelmed — where do I actually start?
[[Beginner's Guide]] → [[Hero Overview and Roles]] → [[Hero Gear Guide]] → [[F2P Priority Guide]],
in that order. Don't try to absorb the whole vault at once — nobody expects you to.
