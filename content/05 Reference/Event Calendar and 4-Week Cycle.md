# Event Calendar & 4-Week Cycle

> [!warning] This rotates — verify in-game
> Kingshot events follow a broadly predictable weekly + 4-week cycle, but exact days shift by server/kingdom age and after major patches. Always cross-check the in-game **Events** tab. Treat this note as "the shape of the cycle," not a locked schedule.

## Two Kinds of Events
1. **Fixed weekly events** — happen on (roughly) the same days every week.
   - Bear Hunt
   - Mystic Trial
   - Alliance Championship
2. **Rotating events** — cycle through a longer schedule with gaps between appearances.
   - Strongest Governor (7-day individual ranking event)
   - Hall of Governors
   - Kingdom vs Kingdom / Kingdom of Power (5-day event, see [[Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)]])
   - Alliance Mobilization / Alliance Brawl (6-day, combines individual + alliance scoring)
   - Swordland Showdown
   - Viking Vengeance
   - Sanctuary Battles
   - Armament Competition (2-day dual-type)
   - Officer Project (dual-type)
   - Custom Selection
   - Combat Medic
   - Champagne Fair (roughly monthly)
   - Fishing Tournament (roughly monthly)
   - Windward Voyage / Treasure Cove / Arcane Trove (chest-based events)
   - Football Fiesta (seasonal minigame event)

## Rough Weekly/4-Week Shape
A commonly reported 4-week rotation looks roughly like:

- **Week pattern includes:** Sanctuary Battle, Alliance Championship, Swordland Showdown, Armament Competition (x2), Officer Project
- **Recurring daily-ish rotation across the week:** Alliance Championship → Alliance Brawl → Swordland Showdown → Armament Competition → Champagne Fair → Custom Selection → Combat Medic
- **Always available:** Pet Chests, Gear Enhancement (daily)

**Important sequencing rule:** once a kingdom finishes its **first Kingdom of Power / KvK**, the event order becomes more fixed. Before that, new servers have a less predictable timeline.

- After KvK: **Alliance Brawl** replaces the usual post-KvK **Alliance Mobilization** — Alliance Mobilization only returns after the next Strongest Governor event.

## Priority Cheat Sheet (what actually matters)
> [!tip] The 20-minutes-a-day rule
> If a member only has 10–20 minutes a day: **5 minutes on Bear Hunt, 5 on Mystic Trial.** These two give materials you can't get anywhere else reliably. Everything else is bonus.

- **Non-negotiable weekly:** Bear Hunt, Mystic Trial
- **High value, coordinate as alliance:** Swordland Showdown, Alliance Championship, Eternity's Reach
- **Dual-type material timing events:** Armament Competition, Officer Project — save the matching materials, don't spend blind
- **Biggest stakes (save resources for these):** Kingdom vs Kingdom, Strongest Governor
- **Resource timing events (don't waste speedups outside these):** Training Events, Construction/Research Events — always hold speedups until the matching event window is live for double/triple point value.
- **Casual/lower-urgency:** Fishing Tournament, Windward Voyage, Treasure Cove, Arcane Trove, Football Fiesta — good extra value, but not worth stressing over if you're time-limited

## Prep Reminders
- **2 days before KvK:** stop spending gems on anything except gear/troops — this is the least efficient time to spend on other systems.
- **Before Strongest Governor:** save speedups tied to whichever category (training/building/research) that rotation rewards.
- **Before Bear Hunt window:** confirm your main march roster is online and ready — late joiners to a rally lose most of the damage value since rallies resolve almost instantly once launched.
