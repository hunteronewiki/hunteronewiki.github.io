# F2P Priority Guide

For new or free-to-play alliance members. Point them here when they ask "what should I actually spend on?"

## The 20-Minutes-a-Day Rule
> If you only have 10-20 minutes to play today: **5 minutes on [[Bear Hunt]], 5 on [[Mystic Trial]].** These give materials you can't reliably get anywhere else. Everything else is bonus.

## Hero Priority (by generation window — see [[Hero Overview and Roles]] for full detail)
1. **Jabel** — free, day 2 login, build immediately (garrison/defender)
2. **Zoe** (once Gen 2 unlocks) — max stars first, garrison foundation for 4+ generations
3. **Chenko or Amane** — collect for Bear Hunt joiner duty, no gems required
4. **Marlin** — 10 shards via Hall of Heroes, feed to 3-4 stars; even 3-star Marlin beats a 5-star Diana
5. **Amadeus** — only if [[VIP System|VIP]]-accessible; his kit stays relevant for a very long time
6. **Petra** (once Gen 3 unlocks) — best F2P cavalry for multiple generations

**Don't** spread shards/gear across 5+ heroes. Pick your **main 3** and commit — see [[Hero Overview and Roles]].

## Gear Priority
- Focus [[Hero Gear Guide|Hero Gear]] on your main 3 only, following: **Infantry Gloves → Archer Helmet → Cavalry Helmet**
- Progress [[Governor Gear Guide|Governor Gear]] and [[Governor Charms Guide|Governor Charms]] in parallel — they use separate materials, so it's not a tradeoff
- Save **Gilded Threads** for [[Hall of Governors]] windows
- Save **Mithril** for planned Red Gear pushes, not impulse spending

## Speedup & Resource Discipline
- **Never** train Truegold/high-tier troops outside a Training Event window
- Hold speedups for the matching [[Strongest Governor]] category day
- Stop non-gear/troop gem spending 2 days before [[Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)]]
- Save keys/currency ahead of [[Champagne Fair]] instead of spending as you earn them

## Event Priority Tier (for a time-limited player)
1. 🔴 **Non-negotiable weekly:** Bear Hunt, Mystic Trial
2. 🟠 **High value, worth coordinating for:** Swordland Showdown, Alliance Championship, Eternity's Reach
3. 🔴 **Biggest stakes — prep matters:** Kingdom vs Kingdom, Strongest Governor
4. 🟡 **Good value, lower urgency:** Hall of Governors, Viking Vengeance, Sanctuary Battles, Alliance Brawl/Mobilization, Champagne Fair, Hero Roulette

## Common F2P Mistakes to Avoid
- Spreading hero shard/gear investment too thin
- Spending Gilded Threads/Mithril without a plan
- Skipping Eternity's Reach because "it's for whales" — it's actually one of the most F2P-friendly events in the game since troops are free and unlimited
- Fully upgrading Exclusive Gear on a Gen 1 hero right before your server crosses into Gen 3+
