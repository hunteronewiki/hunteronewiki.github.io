# Arena & Conquest

**What it is:** Kingshot's 5-hero-team PvE/PvP mode. "Conquest" refers to the stage-progression skill system that also powers your Arena team; "Arena" is the weekly PvP ladder built on top of it.

## How team power works
Unlike rally leading (where only your top 3 "main march" heroes matter), Arena/Conquest adds together the power of **all 5 heroes** on your team. This is the one context where gearing more than 3 heroes has a real payoff — see [[Hero Overview and Roles]] for the contrast with rally-focused gearing.

## Team Building
- **Front row vs back row placement** matters — tankier heroes (Infantry-leaning) up front, damage dealers in back
- **Conquest skill priority** differs from Expedition skill priority — a hero's Expedition buff (like [[Chenko]]'s flat Lethality) may matter less here than raw Conquest-specific skill scaling, so check each hero's Conquest skill line separately before assuming your Bear Hunt joiners are also your best Arena picks
- **Generation-based power scaling** — newer generation heroes generally carry higher base stats, so a full Gen 3 team can outscale an undergeared Gen 5 team depending on star/gear investment

## Scoring & Rewards
- **Arena Points** accumulate from ladder wins and determine weekly rewards
- **Weekly rewards** reset on a set cycle — check your rank before the reset to make sure you've claimed everything
- **Target selection** — picking weaker opponents on the ladder to farm points is often smarter than always challenging the top spot

## Best Hero Picks (varies by server generation — see [[Hero Index]])
- [[Rosa]] is commonly called the "Arena empress" — build her without hesitation if you play Arena regularly
- [[Marlin]] and [[Vivian]] both scale well into Arena due to their team-wide damage skills

## Common Mistakes
- Assuming your rally-leader main 3 are automatically your best Arena 5 — Conquest skills scale differently
- Ignoring front/back row placement
- Not farming weaker ladder targets for consistent points
