# Beginner's Guide

For brand-new alliance members. Point people here first before anything else in the vault.

## Core Loop
Kingshot combines city-building (like Clash of Clans) with real-time territory control, tactical warfare, and alliance-driven conquest — it's not just base-building, there's an ongoing map/war layer on top.

## First Week Priorities
1. **Join an alliance immediately** (you're already in [ONE]OneForAll — welcome!) — solo play is dramatically weaker than alliance play
2. **Build Jabel up** — she's free (Day 2 login) and remains useful for 4+ generations as a garrison anchor. See [[Jabel]].
3. **Upgrade your Town Center steadily** — it gates almost every other system (Hero Gear at 15, Governor Charms at 25, Red Gear conversion at 30)
4. **Don't neglect resource production** — efficient economy is what lets you actually use everything else
5. **Play defensively early** — Kingshot rewards strong defensive positioning over aggressive early attacks; don't overextend before you have troops to spare

## Don't Do This
- Don't spread hero investment across many heroes in week one — pick 2-3 and commit
- Don't skip [[Bear Hunt]] or [[Mystic Trial]] even if you're busy — they're the two best weekly material sources
- Don't ignore alliance events because you feel "too weak" — even minimal participation usually counts for something
