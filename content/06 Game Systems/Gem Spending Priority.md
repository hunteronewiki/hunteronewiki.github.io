# Gem Spending Priority

Where gems go furthest, and where they're commonly wasted.

## High-Value Gem Spends
1. **[[Hero Roulette]] targeted pulls** — the most gem-efficient way to get a specific hero's shards, always in batches of 10
2. **Gilded Thread packs, timed with [[Hall of Governors]]** — see [[Governor Gear Guide]]
3. **Speedups saved for a matching event** ([[Strongest Governor]], [[Armament Competition]], [[Officer Project]]) rather than used on impulse

## Lower-Value / Situational Spends
- **Satin Thread packs** — usually not worth buying since the Alliance Store provides a steady free supply (see [[Governor Gear Guide]])
- **Arcane Trove tiers** past the free portion — compare pack price to actual return before buying (see [[Arcane Trove]])
- **Random gacha/chest pulls outside a targeted event** — lower expected value than a planned Hero Roulette pull

## The Core Principle
> Almost every gem-spending mistake in this game comes down to spending outside the matching event window. Materials and speedups earn bonus value when spent during their matching event — spending them "whenever" instead of holding for that window is the single biggest silent gem-efficiency loss.
