# Governor Stamina

> [!warning] Stub — partially confirmed
> The general regen rate and cap are still unconfirmed, but a few concrete costs are now known
> (see below) from researching [[Cesares Fury]]. Fill in the rest once your alliance has checked
> the remaining mechanics in-game.

## What it probably is
A refillable resource spent on world-map actions — attacking beasts/monsters, scouting, and
similar map moves — separate from the troop and hero systems covered elsewhere in this vault.
It typically regenerates slowly over time, with options to speed up regen using gems, items, or
specific hero/pet skills. Gathering resources (Bread, Stone, Wood, Iron) may or may not cost it —
worth confirming either way.

## Confirmed costs (from Cesares Fury)
- **Scouting a Cesares Rebel:** 15 stamina
- **Solo attack on a Rebel:** roughly 10 stamina
- **[[Diana]]'s first Expedition skill** reduces stamina usage by 10–20% — worth bringing her along for any stamina-heavy grinding
- Clearing the full Cesares Fury event (levels 1–50) typically costs **1,100–1,500 stamina** depending on your power

## Why it's worth writing up properly
New members hit a stamina wall almost immediately and don't know whether to spend gems on a
refill, wait it out, or just ignore it and do something else. A short, confirmed answer here
would save a lot of repeated questions in alliance chat during the first week.
