# Pet System

**What it is:** A secondary progression system where you raise pets that provide passive bonuses, separate from Heroes, Gear, and Charms.

## Progression
- Pets level up through their own material track, earned primarily via daily **Pet Chests** and the **Forest of Life** zone in [[Mystic Trial]]
- Pet growth generally provides passive stat or resource bonuses rather than direct combat skills — check your in-game Pet screen for the exact current bonus list, since these are patched periodically

## Sources
- **Pet Chests** — available daily, a steady free source of pet materials
- **[[Mystic Trial]] (Forest of Life zone)** — one of the best concentrated sources of pet-related materials when that zone is active

## Strategy
- Treat Pet Chests as a "claim every day, don't think too hard about it" system — the daily habit matters more than any single decision
- Prioritize Forest of Life weeks in Mystic Trial if your pet progress has stalled
