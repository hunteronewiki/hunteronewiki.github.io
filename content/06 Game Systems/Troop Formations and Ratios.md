# Troop Formations & Ratios

A consolidated reference for troop ratios (Infantry/Cavalry/Archer) across every context in the game. Cross-check against the specific event page for any nuance.

## General Purpose Ratios
| Context | Ratio (Inf/Cav/Arc) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Offensive rally (Gen 4+)** | 1/10/89 | Heavy archer-weighted once your roster supports it |
| **Garrison / defense** | 60/15/25 (or 60/20/20 depending on source) | Infantry-heavy to absorb damage |
| **Castle Battle attack (KvK)** | 50/20/30 | See [[Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)]] |
| **Castle Battle garrison (KvK)** | 60/20/20 | Defensive lean |
| **Alliance Championship** | ~50/20/30 | Starting baseline, adjust per lane assignment |

## General Principles
- **Early game:** a roughly even mix across all three troop types is safest since you don't yet have strong single-type damage dealers
- **As you unlock stronger heroes**, ratios shift toward whichever troop type your strongest heroes lead — check each hero's troop type in [[Hero Index]] before committing to a ratio
- **Defense should generally skew Infantry-heavy** — Infantry absorbs damage, letting your Archers (highest damage) survive to deal their hits
- **Offense should generally skew Archer-heavy** at Gen 4+, once your gear/hero investment supports the higher-risk, higher-reward composition
