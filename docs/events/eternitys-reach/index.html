Eternity's Reach

Type
Solo timed event (30 minutes), multiple time slots per day
Priority
🟠 High — one of the best sources of Governor Charm materials in the game

Eternity's Reach

eventsolocharms

What it is

A 30-minute solo event where Governors compete to collect the most Copper Ore, which converts into Charm Design/Charm Guide materials (used for Governor Charms — the system that boosts Health and Lethality).

Key mechanics: - You cannot attack other players' cities — it's not PvP in the way you'd expect - All marches are auto-filled with your best troop tier for free — no resource cost, no permanent losses - Because of the above two points: strategy beats spending here. A well-played F2P governor can outscore a poorly-played whale.

Before it starts

  • Recall all troops from gathering before the timer hits zero — if any army is out gathering when the event starts, you can't enter
  • You get a 2 minute 30 second countdown once you spawn in — use it to scan the map and plan your first teleport if your starting area looks crowded

The Winning Strategy (four parallel income streams)

Don't follow the game's own suggested strategy (fighting over high-tier veins and holding the center). Instead run these four things in parallel:

  1. Hunt Cesares constantly — once your skill tree is online, Cesares-kill rewards scale hard; don't rely on the passive trickle
  2. Cycle copper vein captures every ~60 seconds — this is your main income engine once unlocked
  3. Sprint for Fractured Vein spawns — these disappear fast, so mining speed = more copper per spawn
  4. One quick touch of the Peak of Eternity in the final 7 minutes — you do NOT need to hold it, just occupy it for one second to qualify for the separate Occupation Ranking (worth tens of thousands of bonus Copper for top spots)

Skill Tree Build Order

Recommended path: Cesares income → March speed → Fracture Vein → Mining efficiency → save the Level 5 active for regular copper veins.

  • Skip nodes that only boost the passive trickle
  • March speed compounds — it improves every other pillar
  • Fractured Vein nodes convert directly into copper since those veins vanish quickly

Timing tips

  • Slots are typically spaced 4–6 hours apart — pick the one where you can commit the full, uninterrupted 30 minutes
  • This is an active-attention event — a "background" run can cost 100,000+ copper compared to a focused run
  • Pick the same time slot every rotation so you can build a routine around it

Common mistakes

  • Trying to fight for the highest-tier veins against stronger players instead of running the four-pillar loop
  • Trying to "hold" the Peak of Eternity — wasted effort, you just need one touch
  • Getting stuck outside the event because troops were left gathering

Teaching point for new members

Eternity's Reach is 30 minutes of active-only play — recall your gathering troops first! You can't attack other players here and your troops are free/unlimited, so just focus on farming copper: hunt monsters, grab veins on a loop, and tag the center once near the end. Skill and attention beat spending in this one.
