Skills

Sourced from community data (kingshot.net); values shown are at max skill level.

Conquest - Fatal Attraction — Dealing Attack × 224% area of effect damage to enemies in the target area and stuns them for 2s. - Game of Chance — If successful, deals Attack × 300% damage; otherwise restores the target's Health equal to Attack × 50%. - The Tempest — Increasing Enemy Damage Taken by 30% for 3s.

Expedition - Rumormonger — 20% chance of increasing Squad's damage dealt by 40% for 3 turns. - Sound of Silence — 20% chance of reducing the total Enemy Squad's Lethality by 50% for 2 turns when attacking. - Defender's Song — Increasing the total Squad's Health by 25%.

Role

Raw stats reportedly beat Marlin's when Marlin's widget isn't active, making Jaeger a solid alternative core hero once available. Also noted as a defender-widget hero useful for garrison duty.
