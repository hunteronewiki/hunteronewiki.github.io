Governor Stamina

systemstaminastub

⚠Stub — needs alliance input

This system isn't mentioned anywhere else in the vault yet, but games in this genre almost always have something like it gating world-map actions. Fill in the confirmed mechanics below once your alliance has checked them in-game.

What it probably is

A refillable resource spent on world-map actions — attacking beasts/monsters, scouting, and similar map moves — separate from the troop and hero systems covered elsewhere in this vault. It typically regenerates slowly over time, with options to speed up regen using gems, items, or specific hero/pet skills. Gathering resources (Bread, Stone, Wood, Iron) may or may not cost it — worth confirming either way.

Why it's worth writing up properly

New members hit a stamina wall almost immediately and don't know whether to spend gems on a refill, wait it out, or just ignore it and do something else. A short, confirmed answer here would save a lot of repeated questions in alliance chat during the first week.

  • Which actions cost it and which don't
  • Best free ways to refill it (pet skills, hero skills, daily items)
  • Whether it's ever worth spending gems on a refill, and when
